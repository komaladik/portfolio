Project In Detail

To develop an Online Mentoring Management System to promote and encourage students to actively participate in academic activities. To develop a web-based system that will provide the necessary services and academic activities for students.

Our project replaces the conventional and inconvenient method of clearing doubts in class which involves the mentors and students being physically present at the same time.
